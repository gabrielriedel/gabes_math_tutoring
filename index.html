02 — Approach

Steady hands on the wheel.

Ⅰ

Get our bearings

We start by finding exactly where things went foggy — the gap between what you know and what the class assumes you know.

Ⅱ

Build intuition first

Formulas stick when they mean something. We connect each idea to a picture, a rate, an area — so it's yours, not just memorized.

Ⅲ

Chart the route

We work real problems together, then hand you the wheel. You leave each session able to do the next one alone.

Ⅳ

Arrive with confidence

The goal isn't just a better grade — it's walking into the exam knowing you've already sailed these waters.

Where we meet

In person around the greater Seattle area, or live over Zoom — whichever is easier for you. Same focused, one-on-one session either way.

Who it's for

Students who are stuck, students who want to get ahead, and everyone in between. No question is too basic; that's the whole point.
